Aggie Ring Day

Aggie Ring Day is a tradition at Texas A&M where eligible students recieve their aggie rings. The aggie ring is a symbol of hard work and dedication and a lifelong connection to the aggie network. Family and friends gather to celebrate and share the proud moment with their aggie.

Who Is Involved

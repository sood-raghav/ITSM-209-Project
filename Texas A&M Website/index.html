Our group chose the Aggie Ring tradition because we all plan on getting our Aggie ring. Additionally, it is such a memorable college tradition which makes A&M unique. Our group decided on creating the ETAM banquet tradition as freshman engineering majors work all throughout their freshman year to get the major they want. This event celebrates their hard work and dedication.
